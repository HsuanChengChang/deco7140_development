Chang's Work Phase 2

About Us

We are a non-profit organisation dedicated to fostering social cohesion by empowering diverse communities to share their stories, connect with others, and access resources that enhance their visibility and influence. For the Food and Culinary Communities, our mission is to create an online platform where people can exchange recipes, celebrate cultural traditions, and build connections through food.

Project Overview

The Food and Culinary Community website will act as a dynamic, user-friendly hub where members can share resources, highlight food events, and foster meaningful connections. The platform must prioritise inclusivity, accessibility, and usability, while representing the diverse needs of food lovers.

The website will not include commercial activities such as advertising or product sales. However, it may promote community-driven resources, culturally relevant events, and shared recipes.

Community Focus Area: Food & Culinary Communities

  • Home cooks and families seeking easy, accessible recipes
  • International students exploring affordable and culturally appropriate food options
  • Bloggers and influencers promoting vegan, halal, or dietary-specific cuisines
  • Retired chefs and culinary teachers sharing knowledge
  • Busy professionals needing time-efficient meal solutions
  • Food enthusiasts and photographers showcasing food as art

Additional Information

Target Audience

  1. Community Members: Food lovers seeking recipes, resources, and belonging.
  2. General Audience: Broader public interested in food traditions and cultural cuisines.

Project Goals

  1. Represent and celebrate the diversity of food and culinary identities without stereotypes.
  2. Provide clear, accessible resources for everyday cooking.
  3. Foster meaningful connections through sharing and cultural exchange.
  4. Ensure ethical and inclusive design principles are upheld.
