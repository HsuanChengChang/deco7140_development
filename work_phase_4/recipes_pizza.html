Amma Pizza

Cheese, Basil, Pepper…..

Preparation time: 30 min

A soft, cheesy homemade pizza inspired by Italian flavors. Crispy crust, fresh basil leaves, and a generous layer of mozzarella make this recipe perfect for a casual dinner or weekend treat.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups plain flour
  • 1 tsp dry yeast
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 100g mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 medium tomato sauce base
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, yeast, olive oil, and water to make a soft dough.
  2. Let the dough rest for 30 minutes until it doubles in size.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the base.
  4. Add cheese, basil, and toppings of your choice.
  5. Bake at 200°C (392°F) for 15 minutes.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy your Amma Pizza!

Tips

• You can substitute mozzarella with cheddar for a stronger flavor.
• Add chili flakes if you prefer a spicy version.
• Best served fresh with olive oil drizzle.
