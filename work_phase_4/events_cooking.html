About this workshop

Learn to craft classic Neapolitan-style pizza at home. We’ll cover a quick, reliable dough, how to proof it, stretch without tearing, layer flavours, and bake for a crisp, airy crust—no fancy oven required.

What you’ll learn

  • 90-minute dough method (same-day option)
  • Hand-stretching and shaping techniques
  • Balancing tomato, cheese, and herbs
  • Pan, stone, or steel—heat strategies at home
  • Finishing: basil, olive oil, and simple sides

Who it’s for

Beginners welcome—no prior experience needed. Vegetarian-friendly.

Schedule

  1. 6:30 pm · Welcome & mise en place
  2. 6:40 pm · Dough demo (mixing & proofing)
  3. 7:00 pm · Hand-stretching & topping
  4. 7:20 pm · Bake & taste
  5. 7:45 pm · Q&A, tips, cleanup

What’s included

  • All ingredients (00 flour, yeast, San Marzano tomatoes, mozzarella, basil)
  • Apron and utensils during class
  • PDF cheat-sheet and dough formula

What to bring

  • Closed-toe shoes
  • Reusable container (if you want to take leftovers)

FAQs

Can I use other cheese?

Yes—mozzarella is ideal, but you can sub with provolone, scamorza, or even cheddar blends. We’ll discuss melt and moisture control.

Is it suitable for vegans?

Base and sauce are vegan; bring your preferred vegan cheese or toppings if you like.

Allergens

Contains gluten and dairy. We’ll have flour in the air; not suitable for severe gluten allergies.
