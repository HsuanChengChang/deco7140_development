Steak & Stories

About this Meetup

Share your favorite food stories over grilled steaks at Jerry’s Grill. Connect with fellow food enthusiasts in a relaxed setting. Whether it’s a memorable meal, a cooking mishap, or a family recipe passed down, we want to hear it all.

What you’ll learn

  • 90-minute dough method (same-day option)
  • Hand-stretching and shaping techniques
  • Balancing tomato, cheese, and herbs
  • Pan, stone, or steel—heat strategies at home
  • Finishing: basil, olive oil, and simple sides

Who it’s for

Beginners welcome—no prior experience needed. Vegetarian-friendly.

Schedule

  1. 6:00 pm · Welcome drink & introductions
  2. 6:20 pm · Grilling demonstration by the chef
  3. 6:50 pm · Dinner & storytelling session
  4. 7:45 pm · Sharing reflections & dessert

What’s included

  • Grilled steak dinner and side dishes
  • Welcome drink (wine or juice)
  • Opportunity to share your own food stories

What to bring

  • Personal story or memory to share
  • Reusable water bottle (optional)

FAQs

Can I choose how my steak is cooked?

Yes. You can request your preferred doneness—rare, medium, or well-done— when ordering at the event.

Is it suitable for non-beef eaters?

Alternative dishes such as grilled vegetables or chicken will be available, but please let us know in advance.

Allergens

Contains meat, dairy, and potential traces of nuts or gluten from shared preparation areas.
