About this social

Meet new foodie friends over bowls of ramen at Rob’s Ramen.

Whether you’re a ramen aficionado or just looking for a cozy night out, this social is the perfect way to connect with fellow food lovers. Enjoy a handpicked selection of Rob’s signature ramen bowls, from classic tonkotsu to spicy miso, all crafted with rich broths and fresh ingredients. Share your love for ramen, swap recommendations, and savor the comforting flavors together in a warm, inviting atmosphere.

Schedule

  1. 7:00 pm · Meet & greet over appetizers
  2. 7:20 pm · Ramen tasting and noodle-making demo
  3. 7:45 pm · Social time and group photo
  4. 8:15 pm · Dessert & wrap-up

What’s included

  • Signature ramen tasting set and one drink
  • Live noodle-making demonstration by the chef
  • Small souvenir chopsticks set

What to bring

  • Good appetite and friendly attitude
  • Camera or phone for photos

FAQs

Can I join alone?

Absolutely! Many guests come solo—this event is designed to help people connect over a shared love of ramen and good conversation.

Is there a vegetarian or vegan option?

Yes. A miso-based vegetarian broth will be available upon request. Please indicate your preference when reserving your spot.

Allergens

Contains wheat (noodles) and soy. Gluten-free noodles are not available at this time.
