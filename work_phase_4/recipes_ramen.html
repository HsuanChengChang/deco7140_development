Ramen Delight

Rich broth, soft noodles, and tender pork slices…

Preparation time: 45 min

A comforting homemade ramen inspired by Japanese flavors. The savory broth, seasoned egg, and spring onions come together to create a warm, satisfying bowl perfect for cozy evenings.

Ingredients

  • 200g fresh ramen noodles
  • 1L chicken or pork broth
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp miso paste
  • 1 boiled egg, halved
  • 50g sliced pork or chicken
  • 1 sheet nori (seaweed)
  • Chopped spring onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil broth in a pot and add soy sauce and miso paste.
  2. Cook ramen noodles separately according to package instructions.
  3. Place noodles in a bowl, pour hot broth over them.
  4. Add pork slices, boiled egg, and garnish with nori and spring onions.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade ramen!

Tips

• You can adjust the broth flavor by adding more soy sauce or miso.
• For a vegetarian version, use vegetable stock and tofu slices.
• Add chili oil for extra heat and depth.
